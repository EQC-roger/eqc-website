About Enhanced Quality Consulting

Enhanced Quality Consulting partners with companies in the cosmetics, beauty, and personal care industries to develop practical and effective quality systems. Our focus is helping organizations strengthen compliance, improve operational performance, and implement sustainable quality practices that support long‑term growth.

Professional Background

EQC is led by an experienced quality professional with extensive experience in quality systems, regulatory compliance, laboratory and quality investigations, and process improvement across regulated industries. The consulting approach focuses on practical implementation, risk reduction, and building sustainable quality systems that align with modern regulatory expectations for cosmetics and personal care products.

Services

Risk Management

Risk identification, assessment, and mitigation strategies to help organizations proactively manage quality and compliance risks while protecting product integrity and brand reputation.

Process & Quality Improvement

Identify operational gaps and implement structured improvements that strengthen efficiency, product quality, and overall system performance.
